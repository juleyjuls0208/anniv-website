my letter to you

to my home,

i've been thinking about how to write this, and honestly, no words feel like enough, especially after what we've been through. but i want you to have something you can come back to whenever you need to be reminded of how i feel about you. and i wanted to show my love for you in a way i can best express it, using this website.

from the moment you came into my life, everything changed. the world got a little brighter, the days got a little easier, and i found something i didn't even know i was looking for. you.

i love the way you smile, the way you laugh, the way you make me feel, the way you always look so cute. i love how you always make me feel so special. so lucky. your presence in my life is the best thing that has ever happened to me, even if i don't always show much gratitude to it. i love that even after all this time, my heart still yearns for you no matter what. you're more than just a girlfriend to me. you're someone i aspire to be. someone i look up to. and someone i want to be with for the rest of my life.

there are going to be hard days, of course there will be, but there are also going to be beautiful ones. but i want you to know that through all of them, i choose you. i will always choose you.

thank you for being my best friend, my partner, and my home. i love you more than words on a screen could ever say -- but i know this is just the start of our chapter.

forever & always yours,
